💡 Strategy Tips
📊
Always check Analysis first
Before running any strategy, check the Analysis page for current digit distribution, streaks and even/odd balance.
💰
Never risk more than 10% per session
Set your Stop Loss at 10% of capital. Use the Risk Calculator to determine the exact dollar amount based on your balance.
🔄
Martingale is a recovery tool, not a strategy
Martingale only recovers losses. It does not improve win rate. Always cap your martingale steps and buffer accordingly.
⏸️
Stop after Take Profit, restart fresh
Once Take Profit is hit, stop the bot. Reset session stats and restart fresh. Chasing more profit after TP leads to giving back gains.
