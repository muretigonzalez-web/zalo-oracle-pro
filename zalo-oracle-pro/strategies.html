Matches / Differs Strategy
Predict whether the final digit will match or differ from your chosen digit
Intermediate Cold Digit Focus ~90% Differs Win Rate
TYPICAL WIN RATE (DIFFERS)
📌 Core Concept
Each digit has approximately 10% probability of appearing.

Differs wins when the chosen digit does NOT appear — which is 90% of the time for any specific digit.

Matches wins when the chosen digit DOES appear — best used on COLD (underperforming) digits awaiting reversion.
🎯 DIFFERS Strategy
BEST APPROACH:
  • Select the HOT digit (highest occurrence)
  • Bet DIFFERS on it — even hot digits miss 85%+ of the time
  • HOT digit at 15%+ occurrence = strong DIFFERS signal
💡 PRO TIP
Use the Matches/Differs sub-page in Analysis to check live percentages. HOT digit at 15-20% = prime DIFFERS entry.
🎯 MATCHES Strategy
BEST APPROACH:
  • Select the COLD digit (lowest occurrence)
  • Cold digit below 5% = overdue for appearance
  • Wait for cold digit to go 50+ ticks without appearing
  • Bet MATCHES — statistical reversion expected
💡 PRO TIP
Cold digit at 3-5% in 100+ ticks = very strong reversion signal. This is a low-frequency but high-confidence trade.
⚠️ RISK MANAGEMENT
  • For Differs: fixed stake, trade frequently
  • For Matches: higher stake, low frequency
  • Never bet Matches on a digit that just appeared
  • Max 5 consecutive Differs trades on same digit

Rise / Fall Strategy
Predict the directional momentum of price movement
Beginner Friendly Momentum Based ~60% Win Rate
TYPICAL WIN RATE
📌 Core Concept
Markets tend to revert after extended runs.

If the price has been rising for many consecutive ticks — a fall is statistically more likely.
If the price has been falling for many consecutive ticks — a rise is statistically more likely.
📈 BET RISE — Entry Conditions
CONDITIONS:
  • 3 or more consecutive falls in last 5 ticks
  • Overall fall% is ≥ 55% in the window
  • Price is NOT in a strong consistent downtrend
Wait for: 3+ consecutive FALLS
Then enter: BET RISE on next tick
📉 BET FALL — Entry Conditions
CONDITIONS:
  • 3 or more consecutive rises in last 5 ticks
  • Overall rise% is ≥ 55% in the window
  • Price is NOT in a strong consistent uptrend
Wait for: 3+ consecutive RISES
Then enter: BET FALL on next tick
⚠️ RISK MANAGEMENT
  • Use the Chart sub-page to confirm direction visually
  • Don't trade in sideways/choppy markets
  • Max 3 trades then pause and reassess
  • Rise/Fall has lower edge — use with other confirmations

Controlled Martingale
Progressive stake recovery system with strict loss limits
Advanced High Risk Requires Discipline
⚠️ HIGH RISK WARNING
Martingale can wipe your account if used without strict rules.
Only use this with a clear loss limit. Never exceed 4-5 steps.
📌 How It Works
Start with a base stake. After each loss, double the stake to recover losses + small profit on next win.

Example (base $0.35):
Step 1: $0.35 — lose
Step 2: $0.76 — lose
Step 3: $1.64 — lose
Step 4: $3.55 — WIN → recover all
📋 Zalo Rules
  • Use only with Even/Odd or Over/Under strategies
  • Hard stop at 4 steps maximum
  • Never start martingale after 3 wins — pause first
  • Use the Risk Calculator to pre-plan step amounts
  • Your bankroll must cover all 4 steps before starting
  • Reset to base stake after every win
💡 ZALO TIP
With your $10 account, base stake of $0.35 gives you 4 clean martingale steps before risk becomes account-threatening. Keep this ratio.
